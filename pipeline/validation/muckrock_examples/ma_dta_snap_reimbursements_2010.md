# SNAP Food Stamp Reimbursements for FY 2006 - FY 2009

## Request scope (verbatim from MuckRock page)

> The four years of food stamp reimbursement data through the Supplemental
> Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) from financial year 2006 through
> financial year 2009.

## Agency response excerpt

> Per your request, see attached. We were unable to obtain data for FY05 as
> we had a different EBT vendor then.

## Characterization

A statistical SNAP-reimbursement-data request filed against DTA in 2010
and marked Completed. The agency's note that an earlier fiscal year was
unobtainable because of an EBT-vendor change is an interesting historical
artifact that prefigures the more recent Conduent -> FIS transition
documented elsewhere in this repo.

## Why it's included in the V2 MuckRock pool

Tone comparator: shows successful DTA prose engagement, helpful for Phase 5b
when evaluating whether a draft is recognizably "in the language" of
successful DTA correspondence. Not a scope comparator.
