# ID.me verification invoices

## Request scope

Identified on the MuckRock agency listing as a public-records request
asking for invoices associated with ID.me identity-verification usage at
DUA. Marked Completed on the agency-listing page; the full request body
and request date were not retrievable from the indexed snippet that 1c
Layer D returned.

## Characterization

A narrow, vendor-specific invoice request against DUA. Distinct from the
broader `Benefits fraud detection system studies` request also filed at
DUA in that it targets a specific identity-verification vendor (ID.me)
rather than the full ADS / fraud-detection toolset.

## Why it's included in the V2 MuckRock pool

ADS comparator. Establishes that DUA has produced vendor-specific invoice
records under Massachusetts Public Records Law. Useful in Phase 5b for
calibrating expectations of how vendor-specific narrow requests can succeed
even when broader fraud-detection requests are only partially fulfilled.
